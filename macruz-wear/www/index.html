    Our Story

    Macruz Wear is a clothing brand focused in a skateboard style, created by Kaue Macruz back in 2014, Sao Paulo - Brazil. Sao Paulo is the biggest city in Brazil and has a strong skateboard culture. The brand ues a dark style with prints never seen before, prints created by our artists specificaly for Macruz. Currently the brand is not in production, therefore not taking orders
